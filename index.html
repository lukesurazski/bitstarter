FAQ

What is Product Tracker?
Product Tracker is a service that collects information from a variety of online sources on a daily basis for products that interest you. You tell us what product you'd like us to follow and what sources you might be interested in and we'll get you the information. If someone was interested in the same product in the past we can tell you what they found out about it as well.
How's Product Tracker different from a search?
Product Tracker will notify you when something new comes up for the product that you're interested in. It's based on active agent technology that will search out and notify you about a specific product. It helps get new information to you quickly from a variety of sources and saves you the work of searching for this information yourself.
